Setting up Email

Sending email from Node.js is a little bit more complicated than you might be used to. If you see an error message about your email configuration in your Ghost install - you will need to setup an external email provider so that Ghost can send you system emails (for example, if you ever need to reset your password).

To do this, sign up for an account with SendGrid - don't worry - it's free! Once you've got your account details, edit your Ghost config.js to include your SendGrid account details.

That's it!
